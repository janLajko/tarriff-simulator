The articles consist of chassis and subassemblies thereof, whether finished or unfinished, whether assembled or

unassembled, whether coated or uncoated, regardless of the number of axles, for carriage of containers, or other payloads

or other payload.

The articles include, but are not limited to, the following subassemblies:

(a)

Chassis frames, or sections of chassis frames, including kingpin assemblies, bolsters consisting of transverse

beams with locking or support mechanisms, goosenecks, drop assemblies, extension mechanisms and/ or

Running gear assemblies or axle assemblies for connection to the chassis frame, whether fixed in nature or

Landing gear assemblies, for connection to the chassis frame, capable of supporting the chassis when it is

(d)

pintle hooks or B-trains (which include a fifth wheel), which are capable of connecting a chassis to a converter

dolly or another chassis. Importation of any of these subassemblies, whether assembled or unassembled,
